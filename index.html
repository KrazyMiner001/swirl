Physics

I've always quite liked physics, as well as other science. I guess that I just like to be able to understand and describe the world around me.
Most recently I have been studying the general theory of relativity. I've always heard the non-technical descriptions of relativity, with the bending of spacetime and wanted to know how exactly it works. I found the book 'A First Course in General Relativity' by Bernard Schutz which explains general relativity without (too much) prerequisites. You do still need to know linear algebra and multivariable integral calculus, but it is quite interesting.
